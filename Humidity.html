Latitude vs. Humidity

As we can see from the scatter plot, there is no correlation between latitude and humidity. There's a significant amount of cities that have humidity at 100%, and an odd group at the bottom with a higher latitude and no humidity. It appears that a majority of cities have humidity at 60% or higher.
