Latitude vs. Temperature

As we can see from the scatter plot, it appears the closer to a latitude of zero you go, the max temperature gets higher. The higher the latitude is, the lower the max temperature. There does appear to be a correlation between latitude and temperature. It appears that a majority of cities have humidity at 60% or higher.
