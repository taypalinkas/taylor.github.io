Latitude vs. Cloudiness

As we can see from the scatter plot, there is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness. There's a significant amount of cities that have cloudiness at 0%, as well as 75% and 90%. Cloudiness appears to be too scattered to determine where a majority of the data points lie.
